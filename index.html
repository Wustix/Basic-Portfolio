About Me


My name is Wes Roehrman. My entire life has been spent living in the state of Kansas. Born in Dodge City, I currently live in West Shawnee on the western edge of Kansas City. I am married with 1 step-daughter. Some of my passions in life are the local sports teams, Kansas University, the outdoors, working on my home and rental property, and now internet development.

I have 2 Jack Russell Terriers name Halo and Echo. Growing up I enjoyed participating in high school sports, with basketball being my favorite.
